ROHTAK

Located at a distance of 70 km from Delhi, Rohtak is the political capital of Haryana and is famous for its cloth market, dairies and educational institutes. Although not a typical touristy destination, one can still find some decent places to visit in Rohtak for some recreation or fun outings. The city has a perfect place to spend some quality time alone with a loved one-- the Tilyar Lake. Known to have the greenest expanse around its region, the Tilyar Lake is one tourist magnet. If out with kids, the Tilyar Zoo is the right spot to hit. Of course, make the plan according to the weather! Of all the places to see in Rohtak, the Sai Temple is the most surreal and pristine spot to spend some moments of peace and bliss. It is flooded by devotees throughout the year. Another famous religious site is the Gurudwara Shri Guru Tegbahadur Sahib, much revered amid the Sikh community, and one of the cleanest religious places to visit in Rohtak. There also are some parks and gardens in this city, which can be the ideal spots for a fun-filled family picnic.
